About MESERET & AZEB INSURANCE BROKERS

Who We Are

Our Company is a licensed and independent insurance brokerage firm committed to providing expert risk management and comprehensive insurance solutions to individuals, businesses, and institutions across Ethiopia. With a strong foundation built on trust, professionalism, and customer focus, we act as a bridge between clients and insurance companies. We bring hands-on experience in insurance & reinsurance operations, customer service & finance, ensuring that our clients receive the best coverage at competitive rates, with efficient service delivery.
